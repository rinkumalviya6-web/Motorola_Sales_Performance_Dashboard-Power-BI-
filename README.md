# Motorola Sales Performance Dashboard (Power BI)
Interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing mobile sales performance with insights on brands, models, customer ratings, cities, and payment methods.

## Project Overview
This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze Motorola mobile sales performance.
The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales, quantity, transactions, customer ratings, and regional
performance, enabling data-driven business insights.

## Tools & Technologies
- Power BI
- DAX
- Power Query
- Data Cleaning & Transformation

## Dataset Information
The dataset includes mobile sales transaction data with details such as:
- Brand and mobile model
- Total sales value and quantity sold
- Transaction count
- Customer ratings
- City-wise sales distribution
- Payment methods
- Time-based attributes (month and year)
- Customer age groups

## Key Metrics (KPIs)
- **Total Sales:** 769M  
- **Total Quantity Sold:** 19K  
- **Total Transactions:** 4K  
- **Average Sales Value:** 40K  

## Analysis Covered
- **Brand Performance Analysis**
  - Comparison of total sales and quantity sold across major brands
  - Identification of top-performing brands

- **Top Mobile Models**
  - Top 5 mobile models based on total sales contribution

- **Customer Rating Analysis**
  - Distribution of customer ratings from 1 to 5
  - Evaluation of customer satisfaction trends

- **Geographical Sales Analysis**
  - City-wise sales distribution across India
  - Identification of high-revenue cities using map visuals

- **Monthly Sales Trend**
  - Analysis of total quantity sold across months
  - Identification of seasonal demand patterns

- **Payment Method Analysis**
  - Distribution of transactions by payment methods
  - Comparison of UPI, Credit Card, Debit Card, and Cash usage

- **Interactive Filtering**
  - Filters by year, month, brand, and customer age
  - Dynamic visuals responding to user selections

## Key Insights
- Apple and Samsung contribute the highest share of total sales.
- Certain mobile models consistently dominate overall revenue.
- UPI and Cash are among the most frequently used payment methods.
- Metro cities show higher sales concentration compared to other regions.
- Customer ratings indicate a generally positive satisfaction trend.
